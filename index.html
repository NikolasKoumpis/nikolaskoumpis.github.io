Nikolas Koumpis

I am currently a second-year PhD student at Yale University advised by Prof. Dionysios Kalogerias. Prior to my doctoral studies, I worked at Airbus Defence and Space focusing on Attitude Control and Flight Dynamics. I earned my Master of Science from the Delft Center of Systems and Control at TU Delft, where I was mentored by Professor Bart De Schutter. I graduated from the University of Patras with a Diploma from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering.
